What makes a goal "SMART"?

S — Specific

States exactly what will be done and how — the action and method.

M — Measurable

Includes a number or rate so progress can be tracked objectively.

A — Achievable

Realistic given the resources, team and approach described.

R — Relevant

Connects directly to a named strategic objective.

T — Time-bound

Has a clear deadline or timeframe for completion.
